# BidCheck - AI Contractor Estimate Analyzer

**Is your contractor bid fair? AI finds out in 60 seconds.**

Upload a contractor estimate and BidCheck compares every line item against local pricing data, material costs, and labor rates to tell you if you are overpaying.

## What It Does
- Accepts contractor estimates via PDF upload or photo
- Breaks down each line item (materials, labor, overhead, profit)
- Compares against local pricing databases and permit records
- Generates a fairness score with detailed explanation
- Shows exactly where the bid is above or below market rate

## Who It Is For
- Homeowners getting renovation bids
- Property managers evaluating contractor proposals
- Real estate investors assessing rehab costs
- Anyone who wants to negotiate from a position of data
