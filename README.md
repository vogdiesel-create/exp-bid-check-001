# BidCheck

**AI-powered contractor estimate analysis. Know if your bid is fair before you sign.**

## The Problem

Homeowners and property managers receive contractor bids with no way to verify pricing. About 35% of bids have at least one line item 2x above local market rate, usually in demolition or general labor.

## The Solution

BidCheck analyzes contractor estimates line by line, comparing against local material costs, labor rates, and permit data. Upload a PDF or photo and get a fairness score with breakdown.

## Features

- PDF and photo estimate parsing with AI extraction
- Line-by-line comparison against local pricing data
- Fairness score with detailed breakdown
- Red flags for common overcharges
- Local market context (material + labor rates)
